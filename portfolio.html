The Sentinel Details

“The Sentinel” is an application for agricultural sector which can analyze the images submitted by farmers through real-time tracking to detect possible signs of pests or diseases. The system will provide initial recommendations and alternative suggestions to address the identified issue. Afterward, the captured image and its analysis will be forwarded to the agriculturist for further review, allowing them to give expert advice and confirmation. Submitted entries by the agriculturists can also be added into the database which is an opportunity for the app to improve the data within the application which are also verified by the Admin before being added.
